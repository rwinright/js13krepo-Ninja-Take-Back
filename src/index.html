Who will be the first to make it across and back?

Build out the field by taking turns placing platforms/traps/weapons from the top. Hit start to play!

p1 controls:

w: up

a: left

s: down

d: right

e: use shuriken (have to get it)

p2 controls:

up arrow: up

left arrow: left

down arrow: down

right arrow: right

spacebar: use shuriken (have to get it)
